Ishwari Global Tech is an India-based electronics sourcing company specializing in PCB and electronic product supply. We work with a network of trusted manufacturers to provide high-quality printed circuit boards and other electronic products to global buyers.

Our expertise lies in identifying the right manufacturing partner for each requirement, ensuring reliable production, strict quality control, and competitive pricing.

Our customers are at the center of everything we do. We serve global electronics buyers with integrity, transparency, and a commitment to long-term partnerships.

OUR PRODUCTS

Comprehensive PCB Solutions

From single-sided prototypes to high-frequency multilayer production, we have the network to deliver exactly what you need.

Multilayer PCBs

Single-sided, Double-sided & Multilayer PCBs up to 12 layers with blind and buried vias.

Metal Core PCBs

High Power LED PCBs (0.5W – 10W applications) using MCPCB / Al CCL laminates.

Flex & Rigid-Flex

Specialized manufacturing for flexible and rigid-flex circuit board requirements.

RF / High Frequency

Precision RF boards using PTFE(RF) and specialized laminates for high-frequency signal integrity.

PCB Assembly (PCBA)

Complete turnkey solutions including component sourcing for prototypes down to mass production.

Quality Testing

Industry standards including IPC-A-600, IPC-6012, IPC-SM-840, and IPC-TM-650, ensuring high reliability and RoHS-compliant manufacturing.
